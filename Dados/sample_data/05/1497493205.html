Virginia sees 329 new coronavirus cases, now reporting 6,500 statewide

195 people have died after testing positive for the coronavirus

Samantha Smith, Digital Content Producer

Published: April 15, 2020, 9:01 amUpdated: April 15, 2020, 12:40 pm
Tags: Coronavirus, Virginia

RICHMOND, Va. – There are now 6,500 “presumptively positive” cases of the coronavirus in Virginia as of April 15.

[Where are Virginia’s coronavirus cases? The latest from the health department]
